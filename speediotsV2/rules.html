Racing Rules

WIP
  1. Safety equipment: All drivers must wear approved helmets, suits, and harnesses.
  2. Technical checks: Vehicles must pass scrutineering before every event.
  3. On-track behavior: No dangerous driving, blocking, or intentional contact.
  4. Flags & signals: Obey marshals and flag signals immediately.
  5. Penalties: Time penalties, disqualification or fines for serious breaches.
  6. Registration: Drivers must register and sign liability waivers before competing.

Important notes

These rules are an example set for demonstration. For event-specific regulations, organisers will publish a full rulebook prior to race day.
